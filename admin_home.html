About Us

Our platform leverages advanced machine learning techniques to identify and classify hate speech on social networks. By analyzing text data, we aim to create a safer and more inclusive online environment. Our mission is to reduce harmful content and promote constructive discussions.

Learn More

Understanding Hate Speech

Hate speech includes any form of communication that belittles a person or group based on attributes like race, religion, ethnic origin, sexual orientation, disability, or gender. Our system identifies such speech and categorizes it as hate, positive, or neutral, ensuring that harmful narratives are flagged for moderation.
